# ARV Negative Rules

## Absolute Avoid

- no strobe lights
- no rapid blinking
- no harsh flashing
- no hectic pacing
- no very fast rotating objects
- no full dance choreography
- no crowded club crowd
- no random chaotic glitch spam
- no violent flicker

## Style Avoid

- no generic corporate motion graphics
- no sterile stock-video realism
- no over-clean 3D polish
- no bland photoreal cinematics
- no default fantasy art
- no glossy ad polish
- no generic neon cyberpunk city
- no washed-out beige palette
- no cute-only children's illustration when ARV darkness is required

## Text Avoid

- no prominent text overlays unless the user explicitly asks for thumbnail typography
- no obvious subtitles
- no large fake logos
- no watermark artifacts
- no unreadable text clutter
- no generated paragraphs inside image

## Composition Avoid

- no cluttered backgrounds
- no too many simultaneous events
- no busy HUD taking over the frame
- no losing the subject silhouette
- no bright bloom that destroys the main form
