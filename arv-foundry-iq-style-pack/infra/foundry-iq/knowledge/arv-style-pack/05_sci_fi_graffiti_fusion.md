# Dark Sci-Fi x Cartoon Graffiti Fusion

## Definition

This style fuses two ARV worlds:

1. dark cybernetic monk / hooded transmission operator
2. playful street-art cartoon mural creatures

The contrast is deliberate: a serious, cinematic, masked figure exists inside or in front of a cartoon-graffiti universe.

## Core Scene Ingredients

- hooded cyber monk, masked operator, helmeted figure, or space ritual witness
- cyan and red/magenta rim light
- dark gritty texture
- large halo or transmission ring behind the figure
- graffiti cartoon animals / dogs / birds / strange creatures as background relics
- sprayed wall, desert sunset, or cosmic mural field
- low HUD / transmission labels only if secondary

## Balance Rules

The hooded figure should remain the main anchor.
Cartoon elements should add strange mythology, not visual clutter.
The background can be playful, but the total image should remain ARV: dark, signal-based, cinematic, not childish.

## Good Motifs

- flying cartoon dog as signal messenger
- tiny alien creatures in water or dust
- graffiti dog saints in the background
- cartoon rooster astronaut
- desert rave mural fragments
- cosmic street wall
- ARV transmission HUD marks
- halo ring behind monk

## Avoid

- too many characters
- party poster clutter
- childish preschool color palette
- unreadable graffiti text
- overly clean superhero poster look
- loss of dark ARV identity
